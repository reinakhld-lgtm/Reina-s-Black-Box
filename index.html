My name is Reina. I'm 14, Lebanese, growing up in Abu Dhabi, and I'm obsessed with understanding why planes crash and how to stop them from crashing.

It started with a YouTube channel called Mayday. I watched one episode and couldn't stop, it isn't because crashes are frightening, but the realization that every single crash was preventable by better engineering.

So I started writing them down. This is my research journal, my real attempt to learn from tragedy and think about how to prevent it.

Someday I want to build the safest aircraft in history. This is where that story begins.

Reina, Abu Dhabi, May 2026
