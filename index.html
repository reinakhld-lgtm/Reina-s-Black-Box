My name is Reina. I'm 14, Lebanese, growing up in Abu Dhabi — and I'm obsessed with understanding why planes crash.

It started with a YouTube channel called Mayday. I watched one episode and couldn't stop. Not because crashes are frightening, but because every single one had a reason. A preventable reason. An engineering reason.

So I started writing them down. This is my research journal — my honest attempt to learn from tragedy and think about how to prevent it.

Someday I want to build the safest aircraft in history. This is where that journey begins.

— Reina, Abu Dhabi, May 2026
